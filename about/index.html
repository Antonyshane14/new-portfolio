Antony Shane / My Journey

How I got here.

JEE to CTFs to research to startups. Here's how it happened.

Scroll
2023

Cracked JEE

Prepared for JEE with a focus on building strong fundamentals and problem-solving skills. After clearing the exam, I secured admission to NIT Rourkela.

2023

Joined NIT Rourkela

Joined NIT Rourkela in Metallurgical Engineering, but early on I felt my calling was elsewhere. I had always been interested in cybersecurity, so I started taking it seriously and dived deep into it, learning things from the ground up and building a strong understanding across the field.

Late 2023

CTFs & Hackathons

Started participating in CTFs and hackathons alongside my learning. This helped me apply what I was studying, gain hands-on experience, and strengthen my practical understanding.

2024

Competitions & Recognition

Focused on competitions and practical work. Won national and international level hackathons and CTFs, participated in bug bounty programs, reported several vulnerabilities, gained recognition, and built capital through these activities.

May–Jul 2025

Cybersecurity Engineer

Worked as a Cybersecurity Engineer at Briskinfosec. Worked on red teaming projects, a ransomware attack forensics project, built AI-based security tools, and delivered corporate cybersecurity training programs.

Mid 2025 onwards

Research Associate

Worked as a Research Associate at NIT Rourkela under Dr. D.P. Mohapatra, focusing on a vulnerability research program under NSCS with multiple findings under NDA. Alongside this, carried out independent research in security and adversarial AI.

August 2025

Founded Rapture Twelve

Founded Rapture Twelve Pvt Ltd as a service-based cybersecurity firm. Worked across VAPT, SOC, DFIR, and GRC, building the core foundation of the company.

Late 2025–Present

Scaling Rapture Twelve

Scaled Rapture Twelve into a structured company. Built a team, expanded across India, handled multiple projects, and developed strong industry connections.

2026–Present

Co-Founded Zharnyx

Co-founded Zharnyx after identifying a gap in practical cybersecurity talent. Focused on building a platform where people learn through real-world work instead of purely theoretical courses.

Future

What's Next

Plan to scale both ventures further and pursue a PhD, focusing on research that can be translated into real-world products and meaningful impact through my companies.
