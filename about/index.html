Entrepreneurship meets research.

I work at the intersection of offensive security, entrepreneurship, and AI — building ventures and research that improve real-world cyber resilience.

I currently lead Rapture Twelve Pvt Ltd, a DPIIT-certified cybersecurity startup delivering red teaming, VAPT, digital forensics, and AI-driven security products to national and international clients. In seven months we shipped over 11 projects and built a team of 25+.

I co-founded Zharnyx Pvt Ltd to close the skills gap in cybersecurity education — building a hybrid practical-theoretical platform with real-world security simulations that produces workforce-ready security professionals.

As an Undergraduate Research Associate at NIT Rourkela, advised by Dr. D.P. Mohapatra, I contribute to vulnerability and adversarial security research under projects linked to India's National Security Council Secretariat (NSCS). My work targets SCI-indexed journals and IEEE security conference tracks.

My core belief: effective defense starts with attacker-grade understanding. Everything I build — from startups to research — is shaped by that axiom.
