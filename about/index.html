My Journey

How I got here.

JEE to CTFs to research to startups. Here's how it happened.

2023

Cracked JEE

Got into NIT Rourkela. Day one, I knew traditional engineering wasn't for me. Cybersecurity was the play.

Late 2023

First CTFs

Started competing in CTFs and hackathons. HackTheBox, TryHackMe, live competitions. Won a few. Earned my first capital. More importantly — proved I could hang with the pros.

2024

Competition Circuit

Went all-in on National and International competitions. Multiple wins. Built credibility in the security community. Theory gets you in the door. Wins get you respect.

May–Jul 2025

First Real Job

Cybersecurity Engineer at Briskinfosec. Red teaming live ransomware attacks. Digital forensics. Enterprise training. This wasn't practice anymore — real threats, real systems.

2024–2025

Research Track

Selected as Research Associate at NIT Rourkela under Dr. D.P. Mohapatra. Vulnerability research for NSCS (National Security Council Secretariat). Published papers in Offensive Security and Adversarial AI. Targeting SCI-indexed journals.

2025

Launched Rapture Twelve

Took everything I earned from CTFs and consulting. Launched Rapture Twelve Pvt Ltd. Built a 25+ team. Shipped 11+ projects in 7 months. Got DPIIT certified. Red teaming, VAPT, forensics, AI-driven security — national and international clients.

2025–Present

Scaling Operations

Rapture Twelve is now a full-scale operation. Leading enterprise assessments, developing proprietary AI security products, running offensive ops. Research turned into revenue. Defense turned into business.

2026

Co-Founded Zharnyx

Every hiring round exposed the same problem: skills gap. So I co-founded Zharnyx — a cybersecurity EdTech platform. Real-world simulations. Hands-on labs. No fluff. Workforce-ready training, not certificates.

2026–Present

Building Defenders

Architected Zharnyx's full technical stack and infrastructure. Bridging the gap between what colleges teach and what the industry actually needs. If Rapture Twelve secures systems, Zharnyx secures people.
