About

Entrepreneurship meets research.

I'm Antony Shane, building ventures and advancing research at the intersection of offensive security and AI to strengthen real-world cyber defenses.

I'm a fourth-year B.Tech student at NIT Rourkela, leading Rapture Twelve Pvt. Ltd., a DPIIT-certified cybersecurity startup, and co-founding Zharnyx Pvt. Ltd., an edtech platform training cybersecurity engineers through immersive, hands-on learning.

My work is grounded in a simple belief: to defend a system effectively, you must first understand how to break it. I approach security from an attacker's perspective, studying how systems fail under real adversarial pressure, then engineering defenses that hold in real-world conditions.
