Built for real threats.

Rapture Twelve was launched from first principles: no template, no template team. Built a 25+ member cybersecurity unit from scratch — red teamers, malware engineers, forensic analysts, and AI developers — and went live with 11+ national and international engagements within 7 months of incorporation.

The firm's core thesis: the best defence is an attacker's mindset, engineered at scale. Proprietary AI-driven security products are under active development for global enterprise expansion, moving beyond traditional point solutions to autonomous, adaptive security systems.

DPIIT recognition positions Rapture Twelve within India's national innovation ecosystem, enabling structured growth, talent acquisition, and government-linked security engagements.
